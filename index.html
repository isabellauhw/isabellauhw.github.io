About

I am a DPhil candidate in Interdisciplinary Bioscience at the University of Oxford, working in computational systems neuroscience. My research focuses on modelling system-level dopamine mechanisms and their role in perception and decision-making. I am particularly interested in interdisciplinary approaches bridging neuroscience, psychology, mathematics, and engineering.

Research interests

  • Computational models of cognition and behaviour
  • Neuropsychiatric drug effects and neuromodulation
  • Psychophysics and behavioural experiment design
  • Clinical and translational neuroscience
